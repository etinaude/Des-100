Design Phase

Background

We are designing a way to provide a better enrolment experience for students. We are currently at the second stage of divergent thinking in the double diamond design method model. We have already found a problem, narrowed in on it to specify exactly what we would focus on and created some well defined problem statements.



Our ideas

Ideas

We started by creating a list of 100 ideas without filtering them for good ideas, purely just a brainstorm. Many of these ideas were similar such as rating a paper by content, or difficulty, or teaching, etc etc. While other ideas were very different such as show students a video compared to create an online student center. Although there was a wide range of ideas many of them tended to focus in on creating a digital platform which would provide students for information on the courses before their enrolment.

Additionally many of the ideas were not mutually exclusive, and were in fact just component of a larger system. As such we decided to select three ideas which would be good to look at for a prototype. These ideas are:

  1. Show students a video
  2. Online student centre
  3. It explains how enrol is done by video, photo, etc.

First prototype

We picked these ideas because they were quite different to each other and therefore we could dive deep into it each of them. After discussing these ideas we decided to create a brief prototype of ___. This prototype was created by Qiaoyun, by editing a screenshot of the student services online enrolment page. We plan to iterate on this prototype in the upcoming weeks to create a more fleshed out prototype which would give a deeper understanding of what the final product would look like.
